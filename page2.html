What is the difference between stack and queue?

A stack follows a LIFO (Last In First Out) order, whereas a queue follows a FIFO (First In First Out) order for storing the elements.

An array in computer science is a data structure that stores a collection of elements, typically of the same data type, in a contiguous block of memory. Each element is identified by its unique index, starting from 0. Arrays offer fast and direct access to elements, with constant-time complexity (O(1)) for retrieval by index. They are widely used for various applications due to their efficiency in organizing and accessing data. However, their size is fixed upon creation more..
